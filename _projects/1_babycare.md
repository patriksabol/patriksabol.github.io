A comprehensive mobile application for parents to track and manage their baby's daily activities, health, and development milestones.

## Project Overview

Baby-Care is a cross-platform mobile application built with Flutter, providing parents with tools to monitor feeding schedules, sleep patterns, diaper changes, growth metrics, and developmental milestones.

**Client:** Baby-Care SK
**Duration:** 2022 - Present
**Website:** [baby-care.sk](https://www.baby-care.sk/)

## Key Features

- **Activity Tracking**: Real-time logging of feeding, sleep, and diaper changes
- **Growth Monitoring**: Track weight, height, and head circumference with visualizations
- **Milestone Tracking**: Record developmental achievements with photo memories
- **Multi-child Support**: Manage profiles for multiple children
- **Data Synchronization**: Cloud-based sync across devices
- **Parental Collaboration**: Share access with partners and caregivers

## Technical Stack

- **Framework**: Flutter (Dart)
- **Platforms**: iOS and Android
- **Backend**: Firebase
- **State Management**: Provider/Riverpod
- **Database**: Cloud Firestore
- **Authentication**: Firebase Auth

## My Role

As the lead mobile developer, I was responsible for:

- Full-stack development of the mobile application
- Architecture design and technical decision-making
- UI/UX implementation following modern mobile design patterns
- Integration with backend services and APIs
- Performance optimization and app store deployment
- Ongoing maintenance and feature development

## Impact

The app serves hundreds of active users, helping families better organize and track their children's care routines. The intuitive interface and reliable synchronization have received positive feedback from the parenting community.
